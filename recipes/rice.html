Rice Recipe

Description

Rice is perhaps the side of all time. Anytime you make a dinner its worth considering making rice with it. My favorite rice for this recipe is white rice. All you will need is white rice and a rice cooker.

Ingredients

  • White rice, yep, thats it

Steps

  1. Add your rice to the rice cooker with one part rice, two parts water
  2. Turn the rice cooker on and wait...
  3. Enjoy while still hot
