Chicken Tendies Recipe

Description

I'm not going to lie. These are going to be some mid-tasting chicken tenders. Good luck with the recipe!

Ingredients

  • You're probably going to want some chicken cut into tenders
  • Corn starch
  • Canes sauce

Steps

  1. Turn your fryer all the way up to max heat
  2. Cover your chicken in corn starch
  3. Drop those tendies into the fryer for a solid ten minutes
  4. Enjoy with canes sauce
