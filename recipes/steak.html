Steak Recipe

Description

If you're trying to make the juiciest steak known to man you have come to the right place. Our steak recipe will leave your taste buds satisfied.

Ingredients

  • An ENTIRE stick of butter
  • The cheapest steak you can find
  • Whatever seasonings you found in the back of your cupboard

Steps

  1. Put that grill on the highest heat setting its got.
  2. Season your meat on both sides
  3. Drop that steak on the grill for 4 minutes on each side for a perfect sear
  4. Enjoy
